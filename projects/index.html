Acoustic Welding Analysis

For a semester thesis I analyzed the feasibility of using high fidelity microphones to classify welding failure (humping and burn through). If proved applicable in an industrial setting, this could have a profound impact on the efficiency of the manufacturing process, making production quicker and easier.
