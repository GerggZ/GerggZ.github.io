Who Am I?

I'm Greggory, a data scientist and machine learning enthusiast with a background in engineering and patent law. I was born and raised in Minnesota, where I earned my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering and spent four years working as a patent agent. I loved the work—being immersed in cutting-edge ideas and shaping their path to reality—but over time, I realized I wanted to be more than just an observer. Instead of working on the periphery, I wanted to be hands-on, building and developing the ideas myself.

That drive led me to Munich, where I pursued my master’s at TUM and shifted my focus toward data science and machine learning. Now, I spend my time developing machine learning models, exploring real-time motion tracking, and working on various technical projects. This website is a space where I share my work, experiments, and ideas—feel free to take a look around!
